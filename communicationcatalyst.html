Ben Walker Portfolio 2020

Communication Catalyst

Communication Catalyst is a narrative website and set of newspapers that explore the effects of Hurricane Katrina on the communication and structural systems within New Orleans, and how they destructively fed off of one another to create a mass confusion during the storm, as well as a widespread panic and misunderstanding after the storm.
